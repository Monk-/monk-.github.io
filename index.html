Survey Summary

Demographics

Gender

The total number of respondents was one hundred people, of which 82% were women.

Age

The age of the majority of the participants ranged from 18 to 24 years (75%).

Education level

Half of the participants has upper secondary education, and more than one third has post-secondary education

Type of settlement

The vast majority(93%) of the participants lives in town or settlement bigger than town.

Country

91% of the participants are from Poland, 8% are from Ukraine and 1% is from Germany.

Internet Anxiety

In general people doesn't feel anxious about using email
The majority of the respondents don’t feel anxious about using social networks, email or online shopping.
However, almost 20% of the respondents feel very anxious about posting something with their real name or posting something that can be controversial.

Internet Identification

More than a half of the respondents use real name and real picture in the Internet and feel like a part of internet communities.

Internet Security

More than 60% of the respondents doesn’t feel safe in the Internet.
