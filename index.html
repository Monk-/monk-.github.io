Survey Summary

The survey includes 26 questions that can be divided into five parts

Demographics

What is your gender?

The total number of respondents was one hundred people, of which 82% were women.

What is your age?

The age of the majority of the participants ranged from 18 to 24 years (75%).

What is your education level?

Half of the participants has upper secondary education, and more than one third has post-secondary education

What type of settlement do you live in?

The vast majority(93%) of the participants lives in town or settlement bigger than town.

Where are you from? (Country)

91% of the participants are from Poland, 8% are from Ukraine and 1% is from Germany. A good sample is one that is representative of the population as a whole. Our sample is not that good because some members of the population (like men or people living in villages) are underrepresented in our sample.

Internet Use

How often do you use the Internet?

Almost all respondents (98%) use internet often or very often which isn't very surprising taking into account age of our respondents

What are you using the Internet for?

More than 90% of the respondents uses Internet for social network, email, entertainment and sites with information. A little smaller part of respondents (> 60%) uses internet for shopping, internet transaction or online education. In my opinion it's because people are a little afraid of internet transaction or buying something or internet.
